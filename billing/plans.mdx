## Choosing the Right Plan

### Free Plan

Best for:
- Trying out SEO Crawler
- Single small website (< 100 pages)
- Occasional manual checks

<Note>
  The Free plan is perfect for evaluation. Upgrade anytime to unlock more features.
</Note>

### Solo Plan ($19/month)

Best for:
- Individual bloggers or site owners
- Small businesses with 1-3 websites
- Basic SEO monitoring needs

**Key benefits over Free:**
- 10x more URLs per crawl (1,000)
- SEO Analysis for meta tags and headings
- Weekly automated crawls
- PDF and CSV exports

### Pro Plan ($49/month)

Best for:
- SEO professionals
- Small agencies with up to 10 clients
- Sites with 1,000-10,000 pages

**Key benefits over Solo:**
- 10 domains (vs 3)
- Daily scheduling (vs weekly)
- Anchor text and response trend analysis
- 5 AI Reviews per month
- API access

### Agency Plan ($149/month)

Best for:
- Marketing agencies
- Enterprise websites
- Multi-client management

**Key benefits over Pro:**
- Unlimited domains and URLs
- Hourly crawl scheduling
- White-label reports with custom branding
- Custom share domain
- Dedicated account manager

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans at any time?">
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features and pay a prorated amount. When downgrading, the change takes effect at your next billing date.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my limits?">
    - **URLs**: Crawl stops at the limit with partial results
    - **Crawls**: Additional crawls are blocked until the next day
    - **Domains**: You'll need to remove a domain or upgrade to add more
    
    You won't be charged extra for hitting limits—we'll prompt you to upgrade instead.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I pay annually?">
    Yes! Annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly billing. You can switch to annual billing at any time from your account settings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial of paid features?">
    Yes! New users get a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card required—just sign up and start exploring.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
