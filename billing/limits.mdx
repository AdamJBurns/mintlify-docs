## Limit Details

### Domains

The maximum number of verified domains you can add to your account.

| Plan | Limit | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Free | 1 | Perfect for trying the product |
| Solo | 3 | Ideal for personal sites |
| Pro | 10 | Great for small agencies |
| Agency | Unlimited | No restrictions |

<Note>
  Each subdomain counts as a separate domain. For example, `example.com` and `blog.example.com` are two domains.
</Note>

### URLs per Crawl

Maximum number of URLs checked in a single crawl.

| Plan | Limit | Typical Site Size |
|------|-------|-------------------|
| Free | 100 | Small blog or portfolio |
| Solo | 1,000 | Medium business site |
| Pro | 10,000 | Large content site |
| Agency | Unlimited | Enterprise or e-commerce |

**What happens at the limit:**
- Crawl completes with partial results
- Dashboard shows "URL limit reached"
- All checked URLs are included in results
- Suggestion to upgrade is displayed

<Tip>
  For large sites on limited plans, use URL filters to crawl specific sections (e.g., just `/blog/`) within your limit.
</Tip>

### Crawl Depth

How many levels deep the crawler follows links from the start URL.

| Plan | Depth | Coverage |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | 2 levels | Homepage + direct links |
| Solo | 5 levels | Most small-medium sites |
| Pro | 10 levels | Large sites |
| Agency | Unlimited | Complete coverage |

**Understanding depth:**
```
Depth 1: /                          (homepage only)
Depth 2: / → /about                 (+ direct links)
Depth 3: / → /about → /team         (+ two hops)
Depth 4: / → /about → /team → /john (+ three hops)
```

### Daily Crawls

Maximum crawls you can run per 24-hour period.

| Plan | Limit | Resets |
|------|-------|--------|
| Free | 2 | Midnight UTC |
| Solo | 5 | Midnight UTC |
| Pro | 10 | Midnight UTC |
| Agency | 25 | Midnight UTC |

**What counts toward the limit:**
- ✅ Manual crawls
- ✅ Scheduled crawls
- ✅ API-triggered crawls
- ❌ Cancelled crawls (don't count)

<Warning>
  Scheduled crawls count toward your daily limit. If you schedule daily crawls for 5 domains on the Solo plan (5 limit), you won't have capacity for manual crawls.
</Warning>

### Crawl Frequency (Scheduling)

How often you can schedule automated crawls.

| Plan | Options |
|------|---------|
| Free | Manual only (no scheduling) |
| Solo | Weekly |
| Pro | Daily, Weekly |
| Agency | Hourly, Daily, Weekly |

### AI Reviews per Month

Monthly allocation of AI-powered crawl analysis.

| Plan | Reviews | Resets |
|------|---------|--------|
| Free | 0 | — |
| Solo | 0 | — |
| Pro | 5 | Monthly billing date |
| Agency | 10 | Monthly billing date |

**AI Review characteristics:**
- Each review analyzes one crawl result
- Reviews don't roll over to next month

### Team Members

Number of users who can access your account.

| Plan | Members | Roles Available |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| Free | 1 | Owner only |
| Solo | 1 | Owner only |
| Pro | 5 | Owner, Admin, Editor, Viewer |
| Agency | Unlimited | All roles |

### History Retention

How long crawl results are stored.

| Plan | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Free | 30 days |
| Solo | 90 days |
| Pro | 1 year |
| Agency | Unlimited |

<Note>
  After the retention period, crawl results are automatically deleted. Export important data before it expires.
</Note>

## Exceeding Limits

### Soft Limits

These limits show warnings but don't block functionality:
- Approaching domain limit
- Approaching daily crawl limit
- Low AI review balance

### Hard Limits

These limits block the action:
- URL limit during crawl (partial results returned)
- Domain limit reached (can't add more)
- Daily crawl limit reached (wait until reset)
- AI reviews depleted (wait for reset or purchase more)

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a one-time limit increase?">
    Contact support for temporary limit increases during special circumstances (site migrations, audits, etc.). This is available for Pro and Agency customers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do unused limits roll over?">
    No, daily and monthly limits reset on their schedule and don't accumulate. Use them or lose them!
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What timezone do limits reset in?">
    Daily limits reset at midnight UTC. Monthly limits reset on your billing date.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I downgrade if I'm over the new plan's limits?">
    Yes, but you'll need to remove domains or wait for history to expire before the downgrade takes effect. We'll guide you through the process.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
