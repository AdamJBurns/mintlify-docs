## Why Link Health Monitor?

### The Hidden Cost of Broken Links

Broken links silently damage your website in multiple ways:

- **Lost Revenue**: 404 errors frustrate users and increase bounce rates
- **SEO Penalties**: Search engines demote sites with poor link health
- **Damaged Trust**: Broken links signal an unmaintained, unreliable site
- **Wasted Crawl Budget**: Search engines waste resources on dead ends

### Our Solution

Link Health Monitor continuously monitors your website, catching issues the moment they occur:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Automated Discovery">
    Our crawler systematically explores your domain, following every link to build a complete map of your site structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Comprehensive Analysis">
    Each URL is checked for availability, response time, SSL status, and SEO factors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Intelligent Prioritization">
    Issues are ranked by severity so you can focus on fixes that matter most.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Actionable Reports">
    Get detailed reports with specific recommendations you can act on immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
