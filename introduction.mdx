A comprehensive platform that helps website owners, SEO professionals, and agencies maintain healthy, high-performing websites.

## Why SEO Crawler?

Broken links silently damage your website in multiple ways:

- **Lost Revenue** — 404 errors frustrate users and increase bounce rates
- **SEO Penalties** — Search engines demote sites with poor link health
- **Damaged Trust** — Broken links signal an unmaintained site
- **Wasted Crawl Budget** — Search engines waste resources on dead ends
