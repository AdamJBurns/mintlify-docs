## What is Link Health Monitor?

Link Health Monitor is a simple tool that helps you keep your website running smoothly. It checks your site for broken links, slow pages, and other common problems so you can fix them before they affect your visitors.

It’s easy to use, quick to set up, and gives you a clear overview of your website’s overall health.

## Key Features

### Broken Link Detection

We scan your website and highlight any links that don’t work, including:

- Pages that can’t be found  
- Pages that won’t load  
- Unresponsive or slow links  
- Links to websites that no longer work  

### Response Time Monitoring

See which pages load slowly so you can improve your site’s overall speed and experience.

### Redirect Checks

We show you when links bounce through too many redirects or get stuck in loops.

### SSL Certificate Monitoring

We remind you when your site’s certificate is close to expiring, helping you avoid downtime or security warnings.

### SEO Checks

We look at simple on-page elements like titles, descriptions and headings to help you keep your pages tidy and easy to understand for both visitors and search engines.

### AI-Powered Reviews  
*Pro and Agency plans*

AI gives you friendly suggestions on what to improve first based on your scan results.

### Email Notifications

Get alerts when new issues appear or when scheduled checks finish.

### Shareable Reports

Share your results with clients, teammates or stakeholders in a clean, easy format.
