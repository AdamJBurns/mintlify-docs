## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Your Domain">
    Enter your website URL and verify ownership via DNS TXT record or meta tag. Takes just a few minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Crawl">
    Set crawl depth, page limits, and choose which checks to run. Use presets for common scenarios or customize everything.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch & Monitor">
    Start the crawl and watch results come in real-time. Our crawler respects robots.txt and rate limits to avoid impacting your site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Fix">
    Get a comprehensive report with prioritized issues. Use AI-powered recommendations to fix problems efficiently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You'll Detect

| Issue Type | Description | Impact |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| **Broken Links (4xx)** | Pages returning 404 Not Found or similar | Lost traffic, poor UX |
| **Server Errors (5xx)** | Backend failures and timeouts | Critical functionality loss |
| **Redirect Chains** | Multiple redirects before final destination | Slow load times, wasted crawl budget |
| **Redirect Loops** | Infinite redirect cycles | Complete page inaccessibility |
| **SSL Issues** | Certificate problems and mixed content | Security warnings, trust loss |
| **Missing Meta Tags** | Pages without titles or descriptions | Poor search visibility |
| **Missing Alt Text** | Images without accessibility descriptions | SEO penalty, accessibility issues |
| **Slow Response Times** | Pages taking too long to load | High bounce rates |
