## How It Works

1. **Content Analysis** â€” The AI examines each page's title, H1 heading, meta description, and word count to understand what each page is about.

2. **Structure Mapping** â€” It maps your existing internal link structure to identify pages with weak connectivity.

3. **Orphan Detection** â€” Pages with no incoming internal links are flagged as high-priority targets for new links.

4. **Smart Matching** â€” The AI finds semantically relevant pages that should link to each other based on content similarity.

5. **Anchor Text Generation** â€” For each suggestion, the AI generates descriptive, SEO-friendly anchor text.

## Suggestion Categories

Each suggestion is categorized to help you prioritize:

| Category | Description | Priority |
| -------- | ----------- | -------- |
| **Orphan Rescue** | Links TO pages that have no incoming internal links | High |
| **Topic Cluster** | Links between semantically related content | Medium |
| **Navigation** | Improves user journey and site navigation flow | Medium |
| **Related Content** | Contextually relevant pages that support each other | Low |

<Tip>
  Focus on **Orphan Rescue** suggestions firstâ€”these have the biggest SEO impact since they help search engines discover and index pages that were previously isolated.
</Tip>

## Using Link Suggestions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run a Crawl with SEO Analysis">
    Make sure **SEO Analysis** is enabled in your scan options. Link suggestions require page content data to work.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Crawl Results">
    After the crawl completes, go to the crawl results page for your domain.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the Link Suggestions Section">
    Scroll to the **Internal Link Opportunities** section (Pro and Agency plans only).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Suggestions">
    Click **Generate Suggestions** to start the AI analysis. This takes 10-30 seconds depending on site size.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Implement">
    Browse suggestions, filter by category or priority, and implement the ones that make sense for your site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Suggestions

### Mark as Implemented

After you've added a suggested link to your site, click **Mark as Implemented** to track your progress. Implemented suggestions remain visible but are de-emphasized.

### Dismiss Suggestions

If a suggestion doesn't make sense for your site, click **Dismiss** to hide it. Dismissed suggestions won't appear in your list.

### Filtering

Use the filter buttons to focus on specific types:

- **By Category** â€” Show only orphan rescue, topic cluster, navigation, or related content

## Limitations

### Content Requirements

Link suggestions work best when:

- Pages have proper meta titles and H1 headings
- Pages have descriptive meta descriptions
- SEO Analysis was enabled during the crawl

### Page Limits

To manage costs and processing time:

- **Pro** â€” Analyzes up to 50 pages per request
- **Agency** â€” Analyzes up to 100 pages per request

For larger sites, the AI samples the most important pages (based on existing link structure and crawl order).

### One Analysis Per Crawl

You can only generate link suggestions once per crawl. To get fresh suggestions:

1. Run a new crawl with SEO Analysis enabled
2. Generate suggestions on the new crawl

## Troubleshooting

### "AI features not available on your plan"

Link suggestions require a **Pro** or **Agency** plan. Upgrade to access AI features.

### "Monthly AI usage limit reached"

You've used all your AI credits for the month. Your quota resets on your billing date.

### "No links found for this crawl"

The crawl didn't discover any pages. Check that:

- Your domain is verified
- The site is accessible
- The crawl wasn't blocked by firewalls

### "Crawl must be completed before generating suggestions"

Wait for your crawl to finish processing before requesting link suggestions.

### Suggestions seem irrelevant

This can happen when:

- Pages lack proper meta tags (AI has less context)
- Content is very thin (< 100 words)
- Pages are highly technical or niche

<Tip>
  Improve your page metadata (titles, descriptions, H1s) for better AI suggestions. The AI relies on this content to understand what each page is about.
</Tip>
